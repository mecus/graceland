Welcome to Graceland

Established since 2006, Graceland solicitors provide a wide range of Legal Services to new and existing clients. The key to our success is our honest, practical and top quality legal services delivered through our strong and competent team of legal advisors.

Our passion for people is at the heart of everything we do. It’s the engine that keeps us ticking and inspires us to do our best. We’re not satisfied unless we’re making a real difference to people’s lives, and we’ll keep working hard to ensure we get the best outcome possible for our clients.

Graceland Solicitors has Legal Aid franchise in Crime, Family (care proceedings) and Housing matters.

We understand that we are part of key moments in your life, whether these are connected to a company acquisition or sale, a change in your relationships, a marriage or divorce, Immigration issues, your expansion into a new area, or the purchase of a new home.

Area of Practice

Immigration

Our Immigration Solicitors are a team of award winning, specialist solicitors, highly experienced in all areas of immigration law. As a fully qualified and accredited law firm, delivering a global service, we will fully represent your case in close partnership with you.

Family/Divorce

When a relationship breaks down, emotions can run high and disputes often spiral out of control. When you need to move forward, we’re here to help. We’re one of the UK’s biggest and most experienced family law teams, and can advise on issues affecting every kind of family – including divorce, matters involving children and wealth protection.

Crime

We are one of the top law firm that provides a premium service for senior professionals, high net worth individuals and their families. We assist clients and get fair results from Arrest, Police Station, and through the courts system.

Area of Practice

Graceland Solicitors is authorised and regulated by the Solicitors Regulation Authority, ensuring that we meet high standards and follow the correct professional principles and code of conduct.

We Can Represent you

Housing

Moving home is exciting, but it can be stressful. That's why at Graceland Solicitors we work hard to ensure that the legal side of your home move is smooth and stress-free.

Benefit

Graceland Solicitors has a long and proud background of social justice, protecting and exercising the rights of the unemployed, the disabled, those on low incomes or those who can no longer work due to illness or injury.

Traffic Offence

Many people tend to represent themselves in their traffic legal matter. You may not know your rights and the legal language of the court system. If you are facing a substantial fine, increased insurance premiums, or the possibility of losing your driving privileges you should consider contacting an our lawyers for a consultation regarding your case.

Employment

Whether you’re having a few problems at work and want some advice, feel that you’ve been treated unfairly, are facing changes to your working conditions or are facing the end of your employment – we’re here for you.
