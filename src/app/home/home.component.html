Welcome to Graceland

Established since 2006, Graceland solicitors provide a wide range of Legal Services to new and existing clients. The key to our success is our honest, practical and top quality legal services delivered through our strong and competent team of legal advisors.

Our passion for people is at the heart of everything we do. It’s the engine that keeps us ticking and inspires us to do our best. We’re not satisfied unless we’re making a real difference to people’s lives, and we’ll keep working hard to ensure we get the best outcome possible for our clients.

Graceland Solicitors has Legal Aid franchise in Crime, Family (care proceedings) and Housing matters.

We understand that we are part of key moments in your life, whether these are connected to a company acquisition or sale, a change in your relationships, a marriage or divorce, Immigration issues, your expansion into a new area, or the purchase of a new home.
